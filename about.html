About Us

Custom Design Experience Built Over Decades

Since 1992, Heavenly Hands Custom Designs has helped businesses transform vehicles into high-impact brand assets through thoughtful design, visual strategy, and practical customization.

Who We Are

Heavenly Hands Custom Designs is a custom vehicle branding and mobile business design company focused on creating commercial vehicles that do more than move — they represent, market, and strengthen the business behind them.

Our work spans wraps and graphics, food truck design and builds, mobile business concepts, interior layout design, and fleet branding. Every project is built around the idea that design should serve both appearance and function.

What Makes Us Different

Experience matters in custom work. Our long history in business means we understand the difference between something that simply looks good and something that actually works for a real business in the real world.

We approach each project with a balance of creativity, practicality, and brand awareness so the finished result feels intentional, polished, and built to make an impression.

Established Since 1992

Longevity is not just a date — it is proof of consistency, trust, and experience. Heavenly Hands Custom Designs has spent decades helping clients create vehicle designs that reflect their brand and support their business goals with confidence.

What We Believe

Great commercial vehicle design should be strategic, memorable, and built to serve the business.

Design With Purpose

Every project should align with the business, the customer impression, and the intended use of the vehicle.

Function Matters

A strong design is not only visually appealing — it also supports workflow, usability, and day-to-day operation.

Brand Presence Wins

A well-designed vehicle strengthens visibility, builds trust, and leaves a stronger impression in the market.

Who We Serve

We work with entrepreneurs, service businesses, food truck owners, and growing brands that understand the value of presentation, mobility, and visibility.

Whether the project is a single custom van or a fleet branding initiative, our goal remains the same: create something that looks professional and supports growth.

Why This Matters

For many businesses, the vehicle is the first brand impression a customer will see. That makes design a business decision, not just a cosmetic one.

We help clients turn that first impression into something stronger, sharper, and more memorable.
