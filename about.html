Who We Are

More Than a Wrap Shop

Heavenly Hands Custom Designs is a custom vehicle branding and mobile business design company focused on creating commercial vehicles that do more than move — they represent, market, and strengthen the business behind them.

Our work spans wraps and graphics, food truck design and builds, mobile business concepts, interior layout design, and fleet branding. Every project is built around the idea that design should serve both appearance and function.
